Yummy Avocado Smoothie

Description

Quick and delicious smoothie that can be used as a breakfast or snack!

You can substitute any milk for the almond milk. You can use agave in place of the honey for a sweeter taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, sliced
  • ½ avocado, peeled and sliced
  • ½ cup fresh spinach
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth

Nutrition Facts

Servings Per Recipe: 2
Calories: 190

Protein 4g, Carbohydrates 27.5g, Fat 8.2g, Cholesterol 2.5mg, Sodium 44.5mg
