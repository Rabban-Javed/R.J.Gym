Healthy Spinach Smoothie

Description

A delicious spinach smoothie that will make you forget you are eating your greens. You can throw in some cashews for some extra protein.

It's plant-based and packed with nutrients! No bitter spinach taste. Full of healthy ingredients!

Here is how to make the best spinach smoothie! The sweet tart flavor is so irresistible, you will forget it is a healthy green drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 large green apple
  • ½ cup of water
  • ¼ cup raw cashews (or 1 banana and 1 ½ cups frozen mango or pineapple, skip the maple)
  • 4 cups baby spinach leaves or chopped spinach
  • 10 ice cubes
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 ½ tablespoons fresh squeezed lemon juice (half a lemon)

Steps

  1. Chop the apple into chunks, leaving the peel on.
  2. Place everything in a blender and blend until smooth, stopping and scrapping as necessary.
  3. If using the fruit option, try adding the frozen fruit in 2 batches and blending between.

Nutrition Facts

Servings Per Recipe: 2
Calories: 170
